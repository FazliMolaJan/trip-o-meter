Trip 'O Meter

A Modern Android App for your trip.

Get ready for your next trip!

"Trip O Meter" is a modern Android app which would help you on your trip.

It can help you with fuel amount and prices.

With the latest update, it can help you manage and share expenses from a trip.

The app is available on the Google Play Store. The source code is available at GitHub and is open source.


Screenshots

Trip Expenses

We present "Trip Expenses" management with the Version 2 update for Trip O Meter app.

Create your trip by adding members in your trip.

Add expenses incurred along with who paid and who all are required for the share.

The amount each person owes is calculated and updated.


Features

The Trip 'O Meter App assists you to:


  • Calculate fuel quantity and amount required for a trip
  • Manage your trip expenses
  • See fuel prices - Coming Soon (In India only)

Fuel Prices API is being developed by RaghuChandrasekaran
